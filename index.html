01 — How it works

Two controls.
Infinite sounds.

VibraTech strips music interaction down to its two most expressive axes — timbre and tone — and wraps them in a device anyone can pick up and play.

01

Slide your finger

Drag across the touchscreen to seamlessly morph the sound — pitch, modulation, effect intensity. The further you slide, the more it transforms.

02

Turn the wheel

Rotate the precision dial to cycle through instruments — piano, violin, guitar, synth, and beyond. Each click snaps to a new voice.

03

Recognize the sound

The game plays a mystery instrument. Use your ear — or your device — to identify it. Score points for every correct answer.

02 — Features

Built for players,
designed for ears.

Touch-Slide Screen

Precision capacitive display reads your swipes to generate fluid sound transitions with near-zero latency.

Instrument Wheel

Tactile rotary encoder with 20 distinct positions — one for each instrument in the game library.

Score & Compete

Built-in scoring system tracks your recognition accuracy. Challenge friends or beat your personal best.

High-Fidelity Audio

Sampled from real instruments at studio quality. Every note is authentic, every timbre unmistakable.

Visual Feedback

The screen visualizes your sound in real time — waveforms, particle trails, and glow that react to every gesture.

Multiplayer Mode

Pass it around the room. Each player gets a round to guess — most points after five rounds wins.
